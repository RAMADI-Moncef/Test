The Story

Elden Ring is a fantasy action RPG adventure set in a world created by Hidetaka Miyazaki and George R. R. Martin.

Development

Developed by FromSoftware and published by Bandai Namco Entertainment, Elden Ring brings together the best of both worlds: intricate storytelling and engaging gameplay.
