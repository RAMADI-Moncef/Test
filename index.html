Introduction to Elden Ring

What is Elden Ring?

Elden Ring is an action role-playing game developed by FromSoftware and published by Bandai Namco Entertainment. The game is a collaboration between Hidetaka Miyazaki, the creator of the "Souls" series, and George R. R. Martin, the author of the "A Song of Ice and Fire" novels.

Gameplay

Elden Ring features a vast, interconnected open world filled with dangers and secrets to uncover. Players can freely explore the environment, fight formidable enemies, and upgrade their skills and equipment to overcome challenges.
